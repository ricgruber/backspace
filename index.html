What is Backspace?

It's socializing with freedom.

Share your life with the Backspace community through photos, videos, and thoughts. Don’t worry about anything lasting forever on Backspace. Everything you share with the community disappears from Backspace in 24 hours. Here’s the twist: The more popular your photos, videos, and thoughts, the longer they stay alive. Interaction from your friends increases the lifespan of anything you share.
